About us


We at DailyApple have proudly been nourishing Australians since early 2019. We're a small startup company that was founded in the semi-rural suburb of Rollingstone, just a mere stones throw (heh heh) away from the regional city of Townsville in North Queensland.

Our mission

In recent years, the array of food available to buy on our store shelves has been slowly transforming into disgusting synthetic overly processed pulp; complete and utter junk that any sane individual wouldn't even consider feeding to their beloved canine companion. We here at DailyApple pride ourselves on promoting a good, healthy and natural alternative to this ugly food landscape that currently presents itself each week when we venture out to peruse the supermarket isles hoping to replenish our pantry cupboards. Our aim is to catalyse a positive lifestyle change in our society by offering a simple service; one that provides a fresh apple delivered fresh from the farm, to your front door, within 24 hrs on every day of the week.
